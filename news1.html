Knife, Towel, Pillow: Clues Bengaluru CEO Accused Of Killing Son Left Behind In Goa

Suchana Seth allegedly killed her son in the service apartment and stuffed the body in a bag before taking it to neighbouring Karnataka in a taxi on Monday, police said.

Bengaluru: A knife, towel and pillow - these seemingly simple items could help the police solve an intriguing murder case where a mother - the CEO of a Bengaluru startup - has been accused of killing her son. The police have recovered the three items from the service apartment she had stayed at in Goa. Suchana Seth checked into the apartment in Candolim on January 6 and stayed there till January 8. She allegedly killed her son in the apartment and stuffed the body in a bag before taking it to neighbouring ,Karnataka in a taxi on Monday, police said.
